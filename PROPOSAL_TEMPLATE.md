# Opportunity Proposal Format

When you present me with a business opportunity, create a single-page PDF summary. Keep it concise - I should be able to understand the opportunity and make a decision in under 2 minutes.

---

## Required Sections

### 1. Title
Clear name for the opportunity

### 2. The Problem (2-3 sentences)
- What painful problem are people experiencing?
- Who has this problem?

### 3. Evidence of Demand
Bullet points showing this is real:
- Reddit/forum complaints
- Competitor revenue or pricing
- Search volume or trends
- Job postings related to this
- "I'd pay for this" signals

### 4. The Solution (2-3 sentences)
What would we build? Keep it simple.

### 5. MVP Scope
Bare minimum to validate. Bullet list of features.

### 6. Build Estimate
Hours or days, not weeks. Be realistic.

### 7. Monetization
- How do we charge?
- What's the price point?

### 8. Path to $400/month
- How many customers at what price?
- Is this achievable?

### 9. Risks/Unknowns
What could kill this? Be honest.

### 10. Recommendation
Your verdict: Strong opportunity / Worth exploring / Pass

---

## Visualizations

Include simple charts or diagrams **only if they help me understand faster**:
- Competitor pricing comparison
- Market size breakdown
- User flow
- etc.

Don't add visuals just to fill space.

---

## Format

- Clean, scannable, one page max
- Save as PDF to the workspace
- Send me the file

---

**Goal:** Make it fast for Ryan to understand and decide.
